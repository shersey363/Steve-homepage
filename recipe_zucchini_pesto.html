Zucchini Noodles with Pesto

    Ingredients

    • 2 large zucchinis, spiralized into noodles
    • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
    • 1/2 cup pine nuts (or walnuts for a cheaper option)
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
    • Salt and pepper, to taste

    Instructions

    1. To make the pesto, combine the basil, pine nuts, Parmesan, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil in a food processor or blender.
    2. Pulse until the mixture is smooth. You may need to scrape down the sides of the bowl occasionally.
    3. Season the pesto with salt and pepper to taste. Set it aside.
    4. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the zucchini noodles and saut√© for 2-3 minutes until tender but still firm.
    5. Remove the zucchini noodles from the skillet and set them aside on a plate.
    6. In the same skillet, add the pesto sauce and heat it over low heat for about 1-2 minutes.
    7. Add the zucchini noodles back into the skillet and toss them in the pesto sauce until fully coated.
    8. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan or pine nuts if desired.

    Enjoy Your Zucchini Noodles with Pesto!

    This low-carb, gluten-free dish is a perfect light dinner or side dish!
