Salmon with Steamed Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 2 Salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 head broccoli

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil, garlic, and lemon juice.
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Steam broccoli until tender.
